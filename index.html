Randomization

This study is a randomized trial, meaning that you will be assigned by chance to one of treatment groups. In other words, you will have a 50% chance of being assigned to any/either of the treatment groups. Everyone will receive add_circle or a placebo. A placebo looks exactly like the study drug but has no active medicine in it. Neither you nor the study doctor will know which treatment group you are in.

Voluntary Participation

You may decide to stop your participation at any time. If you decide to stop your participation, your relationship with your doctor and any treatment that you receive outside of the study will not be affected.

Informed Consent

Informed Consent Form

In order to enter this study, you have to sign a document called the “informed consent form”. By signing this document, you are saying that you understand the risks and benefits of this study and that you’d like to volunteer to take part in this study. This iPad will allow you to read the entire informed consent form and to sign it. A copy of the form, with your signature and the signature of the study doctor or a study staff member, will be given to you at the end of this process. At any time, you may ask the study doctor or one of the study staff members any questions you might have.
